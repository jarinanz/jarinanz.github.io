Overview

What is CUNYfirst?

CUNYFirst is an administrative system used by students, faculty, and staff at CUNY (City University of New York) to manage various academic and administrative tasks, such as course registration, grade tracking, and financial aid management.

üéØ Problem

CUNY students often struggle with accessing CUNYfirst on mobile devices due to the platform's lack of mobile accessibility and disorganized interface. This issue creates challenges for students who need to manage academic and administrative tasks on-the-go.

‚úÖ Solution

A CUNYFirst Mobile App with a user-friendly interface has been designed to provide students easy access to CUNYfirst on smartphones and tablets. This app allows students to manage their academic and administrative tasks with ease, anytime and anywhere.

How do students understand complex information?

One of the pain points that I found interesting was that students found CUNYfirst's interface confusing due to complex information.

I was curious about how I can improve the interface and help students understand key information (ex: financial aid info and etc) more easily.

Thus, I researched about students' learning styles to find which are most effective for understanding information for college students.

Results

How does the CUNYfirst app compare to the existing platform?

The CUNYfirst app is more user-friendly and efficient than the current platform, offering quicker access to services like class registration, grades, and financial aid. Its modern interface is easier to navigate, making it simpler for users to manage their academic and administrative tasks on the go.
