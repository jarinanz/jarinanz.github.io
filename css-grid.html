HOW TO BAKE A STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE?

In 6 Easy Steps

STEP 1

Gather your ingredients.

You'll need fresh strawberries, whipped cream, sugar, all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, butter, milk, and vanilla extract.

STEP 2

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

While the oven is heating up, wash and slice the strawberries, then sprinkle them with a bit of sugar to sweeten them up. Set them aside to let the sugar dissolve.

STEP 3

In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup of sugar, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Cut in 1/2 cup of cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

STEP 4

Stir in 2/3 cup of milk and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

STEP 5

Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

STEP 6

Once the shortcakes are baked and cooled slightly, slice them in half horizontally. Place strawberries on the bottom half, then top it with a whipped cream.

Place the other half of the shortcake on top, and voila! Your strawberry shortcake is ready to enjoy.
