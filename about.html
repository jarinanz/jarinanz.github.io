About UX Design Resume Fun

Hi, I’m Jarin! 🌺

I am an aspiring UX Designer from NYC, with academic background in Psychology and Design from Baruch College.

I am passionate about exploring the intersection of Psychology and Technology. I use my knowledge in Psychology to address both users and business needs, and create user-friendly tech products for everyone.

I also enjoy collaborating with tech peers, brainstorming solutions to address product problems, and improving design in Technology. As a previous Web Development fellow at Break Through Tech, I had the pleasure of working with my team to create a website using Front-end programming, and gain experience in user-centered design, prototyping, and optimizing user experience.

I would love to explore more apprenticeships and internships to work with UX professionals and mentors across industries, and learn more about improving UX and design in Technology.

Feel free to connect with me!
